Indian Premier League Teams

A gallery showcasing the iconic teams of the Indian Premier League

Chennai Super Kings

Founded in 2008, Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is captained by MS Dhoni and has won the IPL title multiple times.

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians is the most successful IPL team, having won the title multiple times under the leadership of Rohit Sharma.

Royal Challengers Bangalore

RCB is known for its star-studded batting lineup, including Virat Kohli, and has a massive fan following.

Kolkata Knight Riders

KKR has won the IPL championship twice and is owned by Bollywood star Shah Rukh Khan.

Delhi Capitals

Previously known as Delhi Daredevils, the team was rebranded as Delhi Capitals in 2018.

Sunrisers Hyderabad

SRH won the IPL in 2016 and is known for its strong bowling attack.

Rajasthan Royals

RR was the inaugural IPL champion in 2008 under the captaincy of Shane Warne.

Punjab Kings

Previously known as Kings XI Punjab, the team was rebranded as Punjab Kings in 2021.

Gujarat Titans

One of the newest teams in the IPL, Gujarat Titans joined the league in 2022.

Lucknow Super Giants

Lucknow Super Giants is another new franchise that made its debut in the 2022 IPL season.
